# Unit 2: Vectors
- **Vector** = magnitude + direction
    - Velocity, Force, Displacement, Acceleration

# Geometry of Vectors
- **v**  or $\vec{v}$
- $\vec{v}$ has **initial point $A$** and **terminal point $B$**
    - **v** = $\vec{AB}$
- **Zero vector** = length $0$
- Equivalent vectors $\bold{v} = \bold{u}$
